Philippine Eagle Foundation

The Philippine Eagle foundation aims to prtoect the critically endangered species native to only the Philippines. Pithecophaga jefferyi, also known as the "Monkey-eating eagle" holds a great importance to the Philippine ecosystem and culture - it being the national bird of the country. These birds control populations of small mammals in the wild, and is a symbol of strength and freedom in the Filipino culture. Needless to say, the extinction of these creatures should be prevented at all costs.
